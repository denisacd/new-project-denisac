About Me


As you may have noticed, instead of a picture of me, there is a moon. My passion for photography started very early when I had a Philips camera, it was the family camera, and I took pictures of anything that inspired me from nature. Of course, those photos were deleted by my family, it seemed useless to them.

I have over 15 years of photography experience. I started with a photography course and then I attended the Faculty of Fine Arts in Bucharest where I developed my aesthetic sense. During my Bachelors, I participated in various courses and projects on the photo and video side. Currently, I opened my photography studio where I work.

If you want to meet me, have an event or a product launch, you can text me.
